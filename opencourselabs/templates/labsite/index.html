Course Labs

What is this?

Course labs are where you can use our cloud-computing resources via our web console with ease. Academic users no longer have to manage their own clusters which costs a lot of money and time.

It also provies convenient ways to archive and share useful tips and codes produced in your courses.

How to use

If you are a teaching assisstant,
Click ‘NEW COURSE LAB’ on the sidebar and fill up the course lab creation request form. Our service operator will inspect it and create your own course lab.

If you are a student,
Visit the course lab URL given by the professor or TAs, and clik ‘Join’ on the course lab page. You may have to answer some additional questions given by your TA.
