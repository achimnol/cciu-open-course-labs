What is CCI:U?

{% if user.is_authenticated %} {% endif %}

CCI:U (Cloud-Computing Initiative for Universities) provides a comfortable cloud-computing environments, Open Course Labs, to universities. Professors, teaching assisstants, and students have their private web consoles to access and control virtualized instances that are reserved for study and research.

Course Labs (more)

For each granted course, we provide you a small private homepage including web consoles, simple web boards, and private/public file repository. This lets you conduct and take your course with ease.

Support for Hadoop (more)

Not just only virtualized instances, we also offer automatic deployment of Hadoop on those instances. You don't have to think about how to set up a Hadoop cluster. Just one-click to run your MapReduce modules.

Hadoop Source (more)

Course staffs and students can publish their works to Hadoop Source for sharing with others. This benefits MapReduce researchers all around the world as well as you.
